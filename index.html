Our Story

About Dove Kiddies World

Dove Kiddies World was founded on a simple belief: children deserve clothing that feels as good as it looks. From their very first outfit to their everyday essentials, every piece in our collection is chosen with comfort, quality, and gentle care in mind.

We work closely with trusted suppliers to bring families in Ibadan and beyond a thoughtfully curated selection of baby, toddler, boys' and girls' fashion — pieces designed to move, play, nap, and celebrate life's little moments in style.

Because at Dove Kiddies World, every smile begins with something soft, comfortable, and made with love.

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How does delivery work?

We deliver within Ibadan and ship nationwide across Nigeria. Delivery timing and fees are confirmed with you directly on WhatsApp once your order is placed.

How do I place an order?

Add your favourite items to the cart, click checkout, and we'll generate a WhatsApp message with your order summary. Just hit send and our team will confirm payment and delivery details.

Are all products shown always available?

We update our collection regularly. If an item you love is out of stock, message us on WhatsApp — we restock fast and can notify you.

What is your returns policy?

If an item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 48 hours of delivery and we'll arrange an exchange. Please ensure correct sizing is selected at checkout to avoid returns.
